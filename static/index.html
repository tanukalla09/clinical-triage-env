AI Triage Nurse Environment

An RL environment where an AI agent learns to prioritize patients in a simulated emergency room — classifying severity, allocating scarce resources, and avoiding life-threatening mistakes.

⚠️ Synthetic simulation only. All patient data is procedurally generated. Not for real medical use.

⚡ Action Space

  • IMMEDIATE Life-threatening — act right now
  • URGENT Serious — within 30 minutes
  • STANDARD Stable — within 2 hours
  • LOW Minor — can wait
  • ICU Intensive care unit
  • GENERAL General ward admission
  • OBSERVATION Monitor and reassess
  • DISCHARGE Safe to send home
